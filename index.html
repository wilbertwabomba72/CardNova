CardNova

A portrait memory card puzzle where every hidden swap is a plan, and every scan is the reveal.

GenreMemory puzzle
ModeOffline single-player
Levels50 stages
PlatformiOS 13 and later

How It Plays

CardNova starts each level by showing the board for a short memorization window. The cards then hide, and the player drags hidden cards to swap their positions before spending a SCAN to reveal the full board.

Matches are scored only when the scan reveal happens. That timing makes each level a compact logic challenge: remember the layout, rearrange the hidden board, and decide when the board is ready to reveal.

Game Features

  • Fifty portrait levels with increasing board sizes and tighter planning pressure.
  • Horizontal and vertical line scoring with clear combo feedback.
  • Rare special cards that can boost score, moves, or scan chances in later levels.
  • Local progress, level select, star ratings, background music, and a music toggle.
  • SpriteKit and Objective-C implementation designed for iOS 13-supported devices.
